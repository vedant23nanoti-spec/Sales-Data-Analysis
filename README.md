# Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

**Comprehensive Sales Intelligence Platform for Revenue Growth & Team Performance**

An advanced Power BI dashboard designed to track sales metrics, analyze customer 
acquisition, monitor regional performance, and optimize sales team productivity.

  ## 📊 Purpose

This dashboard was built to provide **actionable sales insights** by:

- **Sales Performance Tracking**: Monitor revenue, targets, and achievement across teams
- **Pipeline Management**: Track leads, opportunities, and conversion rates
- **Customer Analysis**: Identify high-value customers and customer lifetime value
- **Product Performance**: Analyze product mix, profitability, and sales by category
- **Sales Team Evaluation**: Measure individual and team productivity metrics
- **Regional Insights**: Compare performance across territories and regions
- **Forecasting**: Predict future sales trends and identify growth opportunities
- **Discount & Margin Analysis**: Optimize pricing strategy and profit margins

**Key Stakeholders**: Sales managers, executives, regional directors, finance team

  ## 📁 Data Source

### Data Overview
- **Dataset**: Sales Transactions & CRM Database
- **Records**: ~50,000+ transactions
- **Time Period**: 2022-2024
- **Update Frequency**: Daily/Weekly
- **File Format**: Salesforce / SQL Server / Excel

### Data Tables Included

| Table | Description | Records |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Sales Transactions** | Order ID, amount, date, product, customer | 50,000+ |
| **Customers** | Customer ID, name, location, industry, segment | 8,000+ |
| **Sales Team** | Sales rep ID, name, region, manager, tenure | 150+ |
| **Products** | Product ID, category, price, cost, margin | 500+ |
| **Leads & Opportunities** | Lead ID, stage, value, win probability, close date | 15,000+ |
| **Regional Data** | Region, territory, target, actual, quota | 25 regions |
| **Discounts & Promotions** | Discount code, % off, product, period | 200+ active |

### Data Fields
- **Sales**: Order ID, Sale Date, Amount, Quantity, Product Category
- **Customer**: Customer Segment (Enterprise, SMB, Individual), Industry, Location
- **Sales Rep**: Name, Region, Territory, Manager, Years of Experience
- **Product**: Category, Price, Cost, Margin %, Stock Status
- **Pipeline**: Lead Stage (Prospecting, Qualification, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed)
- **Metrics**: Win rate, average deal size, sales cycle length, quota achievement %

### Data Privacy
✅ Customer names anonymized
✅ Real sales patterns, dummy financial data
✅ Sales rep identities protected
